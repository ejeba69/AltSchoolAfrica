About Me

Hey there, my name is Emmanuel. I'm a hardcore petrolhead!

However, The most significant attribute I have discovered about myself is that, I am Passion-driven. Passion is my fuel for assiduity. Passion also induces focus and determination in me.

Another valuable discovery is that I have a flair for conceiving and developing ideas. Furthermore, I am conscientious. I love to dedicate myself to solve a problem as carefully and exhaustively as I can.

As a result, I have a preference for embarking on adventurous projects that entails imagination and improvisation. One of such projects is my eBook "Fresh Ideas"

Now, I've always been curious about the "internal mechanism" of computer systems, especially how the software which cannot be described as matter, interfaces with what we can see and touch (i.e. the hardware). I knew at some point in my life, I would satisfy that curiousity.

Nonetheless, I got an exciting exposure to the "behind-the-scenes" of the internet, sometime in 2020, when I had the need to create a website for my motor racing eBook, "Fresh Ideas".

Ever since, things have not been the same for me, as I became bitten by the bug to learn more about the technical aspect of computers and the internet.

The more I learnt, the more hungry I became for more technical knowledge. I started out with WordPress, but it was a bit overwhelming for me. So I tried out the Shopify platform.

Notwithstanding, cost considerations drove me back to WordPress. Eventually, I managed to set up a descent WordPress website for my eBook.

Even so, I wasn't completely satisfied with the plugin ecosystem of WordPress. I wanted more design freedom and flexibility.

My first taste of that freedom, was not from a proper programming language, but from html. You see, I would occasionally struggle with using the text editor on WordPress dashboard of my website.

My goal at AltSchool is to become a proficient backend engineer.
